Slider Buns

Ingredients:

  1. 2 cups AP flour
  2. 1 tsp Salt
  3. 1 1/2 tsp Gluten powder
  4. 1 tsp Yeast
  5. 150 ml Lukewarm water
  6. 2 tbsp Sugar
  7. 25 ml Oil
  8. Butter for brushing

Method:

  • Mix water, sugar and yeast and let it rest for 10 minutes.
  • Mix flour, salt and gluten powder in another large bowl.
  • Once the yeast mixture is bubbly, add it to the flour mixture with the oil.
  • MIx and knead the dough for 15 minutes. Let it rest for 1.5 hours.
  • After the dough has rested and doubled, knead it gently for 1 minute.
  • Divide the dough into 8 portions and make balls by pinching method.
  • Let them rest UNCOVERED for 30 minutes.
  • After 30 minutes, brush with milk and bake at 220 degrees celsius for 10 minutes, covered and 15 minutes uncovered.
  • AFter baking immediately brush the bread with melted butter and let it cool.
  • Your bread is ready!
