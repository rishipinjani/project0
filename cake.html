Healthy Dates Cake

Ingredients:

  1. 1 1/2 cups Whole Wheat Flour
  2. 3/4 tsp baking soda
  3. 1/2 cup milk
  4. 1/2 cup Oil
  5. 1/2 cup jaggery
  6. 1 cup pitted dates

Method:

  • Melt the jaggery with 2 tbsp milk.
  • Beat the jaggery mixture and oil.
  • Add in the dates and mix.
  • Sift flour and baking soda.
  • Alternatively fold the milk and flour mixture.
  • Pour into a cake pan and bake for 25 to 30 minutes at 180 degree celsius.
  • Let it cool for 25 minutes.
  • Your cake is ready!
