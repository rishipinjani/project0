Banana Peanut Butter Shake

Ingredients:

  1. 2 small ripe bananas
  2. 2 tbsp peanut butter
  3. 2 tbsp honey
  4. 1/3 cup curd
  5. 1 tbsp chia seeds
  6. 1 tbsp ground flax seeds

Method:

  • Put all the ingredients in an electric mixer and blend.
  • Voila! Your shake is ready!
